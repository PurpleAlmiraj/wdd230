Membership Level

Membership Benefits

Np Member
NP Membership is designed for non-profit organizations committed to community development and social causes. There is no fee associated with this membership level.

Benefits:

  • Access to networking events with other non-profit organizations.
  • Opportunity to participate in community service projects organized by the Chamber of Commerce.
  • Listing in the Chamber's directory of non-profit partners.
Bronze Member
Bronze Membership is a basic level of membership suitable for small businesses and startups looking to establish their presence in the community. Cost: $100 per year.

Benefits:

  • Access to quarterly networking mixers.
  • Mention in the Chamber's newsletter highlighting new members.
  • Discounted rates for advertising in the Chamber's promotional materials.
Silver Member
Silver Membership is ideal for established businesses seeking to expand their reach and influence in the local market. Cost: $250 per year.

Benefits:

  • Priority placement on the Chamber's website directory, with a spotlight feature on the homepage.
  • Invitation to exclusive training workshops and seminars hosted by industry experts.
  • Discounts on Chamber-sponsored events and programs.
Gold Member
Gold Membership is the premier level of membership, offering maximum visibility and access to resources for large corporations and industry leaders. Cost: $500 per year.

Benefits:

  • Featured profile in the Chamber's monthly newsletter, reaching thousands of subscribers.
  • VIP access to Chamber-hosted events, including meet-and-greets with prominent community leaders.
  • Complimentary advertising space on the Chamber's website for promotional campaigns.
