Rexburg's story is one woven with threads of pioneering spirit and perseverance. Founded in the late 19th century by Mormon settlers, the city quickly flourished as a center of agriculture and commerce in the region. Its strategic location along the banks of the Teton River provided fertile ground for farming, while the advent of the railroad further fueled its growth as a bustling hub of trade and industry.

Throughout its history, Rexburg has weathered its share of challenges, including economic downturns and natural disasters. Yet, time and again, the indomitable spirit of its residents has prevailed, driving the city forward with a relentless determination to thrive and prosper.

Today, Rexburg boasts a diverse demographic landscape that reflects the rich tapestry of cultures and backgrounds that call the city home. From descendants of early settlers to transplants drawn by opportunities in education and business, Rexburg's population is a vibrant mosaic of ethnicities, religions, and traditions.

At the heart of this diversity lies Brigham Young University-Idaho (BYU-Idaho), a world-renowned institution that attracts students from across the globe. With its emphasis on academic excellence, community engagement, and religious values, BYU-Idaho serves as a beacon of learning and enlightenment, enriching the cultural fabric of Rexburg and fostering a spirit of inclusivity and understanding among its inhabitants.

In recent years, Rexburg has experienced a renaissance of sorts, with a resurgence of interest in local arts, culture, and entrepreneurship. From artisanal craft fairs to live music festivals, the city buzzes with activity year-round, offering residents and visitors a plethora of opportunities to connect, celebrate, and create lasting memories.

At the same time, Rexburg remains deeply rooted in its traditions and values, with a strong emphasis on community service, civic engagement, and philanthropy. Whether it's organizing food drives for the less fortunate or rallying together to support local charities and causes, the people of Rexburg exemplify the true spirit of altruism and compassion, enriching the lives of those around them and strengthening the bonds of community.

In conclusion, Rexburg stands as a shining example of resilience, diversity, and vibrancy in the heart of Idaho. From its storied past to its dynamic present, the city continues to evolve and thrive, guided by a shared vision of progress, unity, and prosperity. Whether you're exploring its rich history, immersing yourself in its diverse cultural tapestry, or experiencing the excitement of its current events, Rexburg offers a truly unforgettable experience that celebrates the best of what it means to be part of a vibrant and thriving community.

Current Events

Rexburg Harvest Festival

Celebrate the bounty of the season at the annual Rexburg Harvest Festival! Held at the city's historic Main Street, this lively event features pumpkin carving contests, hayrides, live music performances, and a bustling farmers' market showcasing the region's finest produce and artisanal goods. Families and friends come together to enjoy the crisp autumn air and revel in the festive atmosphere of this beloved community tradition.

Rexburg Tech Expo

Calling all tech enthusiasts and innovators! The Rexburg Tech Expo is back, showcasing the latest advancements in technology and digital innovation. Hosted at the state-of-the-art Rexburg Innovation Center, this two-day event features keynote speakers, interactive workshops, and product demonstrations from leading tech companies and startups. From artificial intelligence and virtual reality to blockchain and cybersecurity, explore the cutting-edge technologies shaping the future of industry and society.

Rexburg Film Festival

Lights, camera, action! The Rexburg Film Festival returns for its annual showcase of cinematic excellence. Held at the historic Rexburg Theater, this prestigious event celebrates the art of storytelling through film, featuring screenings of independent films, documentaries, and student productions from around the world. Join filmmakers, industry professionals, and film enthusiasts alike as they come together to celebrate creativity, diversity, and the magic of cinema.
